The Best Movies According to Josue

My top 3 movies of all time.

What Dreams May Come

This is a fantasy movie starring Robin Williams and deals what the topics of suicide and the afterlife.

Hamlet

This movie interpretation of Shakespear's play Hamlet stars Mel Gibson and Glen Close. I studied this movie in college and watched it repeatedly, since I've never read the play!

Eyes Wides Shut

This is a Stanley Kubrick movie. It stars Nicole Kidman and Tom Cruise. I wrote about Kubrick's work in my undergraduate literature studies and fell in love with his style of moviemaking.
